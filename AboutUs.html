About Us

Southern Methodist University

Southern Methodist University is a private research university in University Park, Texas, with satellite campuses in Plano, Texas and Taos, New Mexico. SMU was founded on April 17, 1911 by the Methodist Episcopal Church, South—now part of the United Methodist Church—in partnership with Dallas civic leaders.

Colorado School of Mines

The Colorado School of Mines is a public research university in Golden, Colorado, founded in 1874 under the auspices of the Episcopal Church, and with support from the territorial government. It became a state institution two years later when Colorado gained statehood. It grants degrees primarily in engineering and the applied sciences, with a focus on earth, energy and the environment. It offers graduate degrees in Operations Research with Engineering upon which the work presented here is based.
